REU Past Student's Work

Each participant in our program produces a paper describing their results after just eight weeks of work. The research represented by these papers was made possible by NSF-REU grants DMS-9987955, DMS-0139426, DMS-0453605, DMS-0850959, DMS-1156608, DMS-1461286, DMS-1758020 as well as the generous support of California State University, San Bernardino.

Below are some of the papers from each of the topics we have studied: Geometry, and Knot Theory. Although combinatorics is not necessarily one of the topics we still offer as part of this REU, we list the work of our former participants in that field as well.
